The rotate() method rotates an element clockwise or counter-clockwise.

The scale() Method

The scale() method increases or decreases the size of an element.

The skew() Method

The skew() method skews an element along the X and Y-axis by the given angles.

This a normal div element.
Skewed 20 degrees along the X-axis.
Skewed 10 degrees along the Y-axis.
Skewed 20 degrees along the X-axis, and 10 degrees along the Y-axis.
